CONCEPT 1: "THE SHADOW NETWORK" (RECOMMENDED)
=========================================================

VISUAL DESCRIPTION:
- Dark, moody color palette: deep navy blue, charcoal black, gold accents
- Central image: A silhouette of a man in a suit, rendered in solid black,
  standing at the center of a web/network of thin gold lines
- The gold lines connect to small circles/nodes, suggesting a network map
- Background: Faintly visible cityscape (Manhattan skyline) in very dark blue
- The silhouette casts an impossibly long shadow downward
- Title "SHADOWS OF POWER" in large, bold, sans-serif white font (uppercase)
- Subtitle in smaller gold font below
- Author name at bottom in white

WHY IT WORKS:
- Immediately communicates themes: power, secrecy, networks
- Clean and readable at thumbnail size
- Gold + navy = premium nonfiction feel
- Silhouette maintains mystery (no specific face needed)
- Network lines hint at conspiracy without being sensationalist

PROMPT FOR AI GENERATION:
"Book cover design, dark navy blue and black background with Manhattan 
skyline faintly visible, black silhouette of a man in suit at center, 
connected by thin gold lines to network nodes like a spider web, 
long shadow cast downward, premium nonfiction true crime aesthetic, 
cinematic lighting, minimalist and sophisticated"

=========================================================
CONCEPT 2: "THE ISLAND"  
=========================================================

VISUAL DESCRIPTION:
- Aerial/bird's eye view of a small tropical island in dark ocean water
- Color treatment: heavily desaturated, almost black and white, with 
  a single spot of blue-green for the ocean
- The island is small, isolated, ominous — not paradise-like
- A single tiny building (suggesting the temple) visible on the island
- Red/crimson title text overlaid: "SHADOWS OF POWER"
- Dark vignette around edges creating a surveillance-camera feel
- Subtitle in white below title
- Tagline at top: "Every institution failed. This book explains why."

WHY IT WORKS:
- The island is the most iconic visual element of the Epstein story
- Desaturated treatment prevents it from looking like a travel book
- Surveillance-camera vignette adds menace
- High recognition factor for anyone familiar with the case
- Red title on dark image = high contrast, readable at any size

PROMPT FOR AI GENERATION:
"Aerial photograph of a small isolated tropical island in dark ocean, 
heavily desaturated almost black and white, single small building 
visible, dark vignette edges like surveillance camera footage, 
ominous atmosphere, not paradise-like, true crime book cover aesthetic"

=========================================================
CONCEPT 3: "THE REDACTED FILE"
=========================================================

VISUAL DESCRIPTION:
- Background: Texture of an aged government document/legal filing
- Most of the text on the document is blacked out (redacted) with 
  heavy black bars
- A few fragmentary words visible through the redaction: "trafficking," 
  "minor," "immunity," "classified"
- Title "SHADOWS OF POWER" written as if stamped in red ink across 
  the document
- Author name appears at bottom like a signature line
- A small "CONFIDENTIAL" watermark diagonal across the page

WHY IT WORKS:
- Directly references the sealed/redacted documents at the heart of the case
- Provocative — the reader wonders what's under the black bars
- Unique visual approach, stands out from other true crime covers
- Government document aesthetic signals serious, investigative content
- Works at any size because the redaction bars are high-contrast

PROMPT FOR AI GENERATION:
"Book cover designed as a classified government document, aged paper 
texture, heavy black redaction bars covering most text, fragmentary 
words visible like trafficking and immunity, red stamp text reading 
SHADOWS OF POWER, CONFIDENTIAL watermark, legal document aesthetic, 
investigative nonfiction true crime"

=========================================================
CONCEPT 4: "TWO FACES OF JUSTICE"
=========================================================

VISUAL DESCRIPTION:
- Split image, divided vertically
- Left half: A grand courthouse facade (columns, steps) in warm golden light
- Right half: The same courthouse, but in shadow/darkness, crumbling or decayed
- The division line is sharp and dramatic
- Title spans both halves in bold white letters with slight shadow
- Subtitle below in a contrasting color (gold or red)
- Overall feel: the duality of justice — the promise vs. the reality

WHY IT WORKS:
- Visually communicates the book's core thesis (justice as illusion)
- Architectural imagery = gravitas and serious nonfiction
- Split-image concept is eye-catching and memorable
- Works well in both color and grayscale
- Clean design with strong readability

PROMPT FOR AI GENERATION:
"Book cover with split image divided vertically, left half shows 
grand neoclassical courthouse in warm golden light, right half shows 
same courthouse in shadow and decay, dramatic contrast, true crime 
investigative nonfiction aesthetic, bold clean typography space at top"

PRINT SPECIFICATIONS (for final cover)
=========================================================

Format: 6" x 9" (standard trade paperback)
Resolution: 300 DPI minimum
Color space: CMYK for print, RGB for digital
Bleed: 0.125" on all sides
Spine width: Calculate based on page count × paper thickness
File format: PDF (print), JPEG/PNG (digital)
Amazon KDP cover template: https://kdp.amazon.com/cover-calculator
