=========================================================
RISK ANALYSIS
=========================================================

LEGAL RISKS:
- Automated publishing without human review could result in 
  publishing content that violates platform terms of service
- Content based on real events involving named individuals 
  requires careful legal review for defamation risk
- Both KDP and Draft2Digital have content policies that 
  require human acknowledgment

TECHNICAL RISKS:
- Browser automation of publishing platforms violates their 
  Terms of Service
- Accounts can be permanently banned for bot-detected activity
- CAPTCHA and 2FA systems block automated submissions
- Platform UI changes can break automation scripts without warning

ETHICAL RISKS:
- Publishing is a final, public act that should have human oversight
- The "publish" button should always require human confirmation
- Pricing, distribution rights, and royalty settings have financial 
  implications that require human decision-making
