## Cover Concept 1: "The Chess Master" (Iconic Symbol)
**Filename:** Cover_1_Chess_Master.png

**Prompt for Gemini:**
Create a professional book cover for a nonfiction political biography. Portrait orientation, vertical book cover format, aspect ratio 2:3. ## Cover Concept 4: "The Files" (Documentary/Evidence)
**Filename:** Cover_4_Files.png

**Prompt for Gemini:**
Create a professional book cover for a nonfiction political biography. Portrait orientation, vertical book cover format, aspect ratio 2:3. Design: An overhead view of a dark wooden desk covered with declassified documents, some with "TOP SECRET" stamps and heavy black redaction bars. A pair of wire-rimmed glasses sits on top of one document. A fountain pen rests beside them. One document corner is slightly curled. The desk is lit by a single desk lamp from above-right, creating dramatic shadows. Some text on the documents is visible but blurred except for key words: "CAMBODIA," "CHILE," "DETENTE." Color palette: dark walnut, cream paper, black ink, red stamps. Atmospheric, investigative, documentary. Typography: Title "ARCHITECT OF POWER" in bold white serif across the top third. Subtitle "Henry Kissinger and the Price of World Order" in smaller off-white. Author name "MICHAEL RODRIGUEZ" in white at the bottom. Premium nonfiction book cover.

## Typography Notes for All Covers
- Title font: Bold serif (Playfair Display, Crimson, or similar)
- Title placement: upper third for thumbnail visibility
- Author name: always at bottom, smaller but legible
- Subtitle: between title and center
- No more than 3 font sizes per cover
- Must be readable at Amazon thumbnail size (~120px wide)
