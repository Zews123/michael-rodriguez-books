About the Author

Michael Rodriguez

A renowned investigative author and economic analyst specializing in money laundering networks, financial crime investigation, and the global shadow economy. He combines economic theory, forensic analysis, and investigative journalism to expose connections between financial institutions, technological innovation, and broader economic corruption.

With over fifteen years of experience in economic analysis and financial crime investigation, Rodriguez specializes in examining how global power structures operate through offshore networks, technological systems, and shadow financial markets. His work bridges academic research and accessible storytelling.
