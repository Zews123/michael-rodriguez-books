### Chapter 11: The Conspiracy Evaluation Framework (~3,300 words)
**"How to Think About What You Can't See"**

**Hook type:** Provocative question  
*"Here's a test. Two statements: 'The CIA secretly dosed American citizens with LSD for twenty years.' And: 'A secret cabal of elites drinks children's blood to stay young.' One is documented fact. One is QAnon. Can you tell which is which without looking it up? If you hesitated — this chapter is for you."*

**Protagonist:** The reader — positioned as active participant

- The five-point Conspiracy Evaluation Framework:
  1. **Documentation:** Is there primary source evidence (declassified files, court records, FOIA documents)?
  2. **Specificity:** Are there named individuals, specific dates, and concrete actions?
  3. **Verification:** Has it been confirmed by multiple independent sources?
  4. **Acknowledgment:** Has a government investigation or official source acknowledged it?
  5. **Mechanism:** Is there a falsifiable, understandable mechanism (not magic or omnipotent coordination)?
